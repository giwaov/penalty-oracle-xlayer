How It Works

New fan guide
1 Connect wallet

Use OKX Wallet or any EVM wallet on X Layer mainnet. You need a small amount of OKB for gas.

2 Pick a squad

Choose the country you want to represent. Your squad unlocks daily penalties.

3 Take your shot

Aim left, center, or right. Each kick is one wallet transaction, then the AI keeper reveals the on-chain result.

4 Climb the table

Each wallet gets five daily penalties. Goals and streaks add points to your wallet rank, today's race, and country leaderboard.

Every squad join and penalty is recorded on X Layer. One wallet gets five shots per day, registered wallets compete on the player leaderboard, and the Oracle Agent creates a shareable report after every shot.
